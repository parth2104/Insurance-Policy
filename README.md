 # Insurance Policy Analysis Report (Power BI)

##  Project Overview

This project focuses on building an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze insurance policy data and extract meaningful business insights. The dashboard provides a centralized view of claims, premiums, customer demographics, and policy performance, enabling better decision-making through data visualization.

---

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to transform raw insurance data into actionable insights that can support business decisions. The dashboard is designed to help stakeholders monitor key performance indicators, understand customer behavior, evaluate product performance, and improve overall operational efficiency.

---

## Problem Statement

Insurance companies often deal with large volumes of data related to policies, claims, and customers. However, this data is typically scattered and difficult to interpret, making it challenging to:

* Track claim processing efficiently
* Identify high-performing insurance products
* Understand customer behavior and risk patterns
* Monitor policy activity and retention

This project addresses these challenges by creating a unified dashboard that simplifies analysis and improves visibility across key business areas.

---

## Dashboard Features

* Claim status analysis (Rejected, Settled, Pending)
* Premium distribution by policy type
* Claim amount trends across age groups
* Customer segmentation (gender distribution)
* Active vs inactive policy tracking
* Interactive filters for dynamic analysis

---
## Row-Level Security (RLS)
Row-Level Security (RLS) is implemented to restrict data access based on user roles. It ensures that users can only view data relevant to them, such as specific regions or segments. This enhances data security and simulates real-world access control in insurance systems.

---

## Business Use & Impact

This dashboard is designed to transform raw insurance data into meaningful insights by providing a centralized and interactive view of key business operations. It enables stakeholders to make faster, data-driven decisions without relying on manual analysis.

### Operational Efficiency

The dashboard improves operational efficiency by allowing teams to monitor the entire claims lifecycle in real time. By clearly visualizing the distribution of rejected, settled, and pending claims, it becomes easier to identify process bottlenecks and delays. This helps organizations streamline workflows, reduce turnaround time, and enhance overall service quality.

###  Revenue & Product Strategy

It provides strong support for revenue optimization by highlighting how different policy types contribute to premium income. Decision-makers can quickly identify high-performing products and focus on areas that generate the most value. This insight also helps in refining pricing strategies, improving product offerings, and planning targeted marketing campaigns.

### Customer Insights

The dashboard delivers valuable customer insights by analyzing demographic patterns such as age group distribution. It helps businesses understand which customer segments are most active, which groups contribute more to claims, and where opportunities or risks exist. These insights support better customer segmentation and enable more personalized services.

### Risk Management

From a risk management perspective, the dashboard identifies trends in claim amounts across different customer segments. This makes it easier to detect high-risk groups early and adjust underwriting strategies accordingly. As a result, organizations can proactively reduce potential losses and maintain financial stability.

### Customer Retention

The dashboard also plays an important role in improving customer retention by tracking the proportion of active versus inactive policies. This provides visibility into customer engagement and helps identify potential churn. Businesses can use this information to design targeted retention strategies and improve policy renewal rates.

###  Overall Impact

Overall, this dashboard converts complex insurance data into actionable insights, helping organizations improve operational performance, optimize revenue strategies, manage risk effectively, and enhance customer satisfaction.
